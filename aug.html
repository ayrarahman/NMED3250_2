AUGUST

Oh August, the last month of summer. The month where everyone is trying to get in their last bit of fun before school starts again... and what is more fun than reading?

There is no one list that is the absolute dictation of the best books, so this page will show off multiple lists from multiple
sites that highlights all different demographics!
