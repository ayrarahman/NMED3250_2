JANUARY

Ahhh January, the start of the year, a time of new beginnings. What is better to start off the year than with
some good books! Let’s take a look at what everyone thought were the best books of January 2023.

There is no one list that is the absolute dictation of the best books, so this page will show off multiple lists from multiple
sites that highlights all different demographics!
